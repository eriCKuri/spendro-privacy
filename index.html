Spendro — Privacy Policy

✓ 100% Offline · No Data Collection · No Account

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Overview

Spendro is a fully offline personal finance assistant built for privacy. We do not collect, store, transmit, share, or sell any of your personal data. Everything you enter into Spendro stays on your device only.

Camera Permission

Spendro requests camera access only to allow you to photograph receipts and invoices for transaction recording. Images are processed entirely on your device using on-device text recognition. Photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored anywhere outside your device.

Photo Library Permission

Spendro requests photo library access to allow you to select existing images of receipts or bank statement screenshots. These images are processed on your device only and never leave your device.

Notifications Permission

Spendro uses local notifications only. These are generated on your device to remind you to log daily transactions, send daily spending summaries, and alert you about upcoming payment dates. No notification data is sent to any external server.

Bank Notification Detection (Optional Feature)

Spendro includes an optional feature that can automatically detect transactions from bank SMS notifications. This feature is disabled by default and requires your explicit permission to enable. When enabled, Spendro reads the text of incoming notifications from banking apps only. This processing happens entirely on your device. No notification content is ever transmitted, uploaded, or shared with anyone. You must confirm every detected transaction before it is saved. You can disable this feature at any time in Settings.

Calendar Permission

If granted, calendar access is used only to create local reminders for your income payment dates. Calendar data is never read for any other purpose and is never transmitted anywhere.

Local Data Storage

All your financial data including income sources, transactions, budgets, categories, and reports is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. No data is ever sent to any server, cloud service, or third party under any circumstances.

No Account Required

Spendro requires no account, email address, phone number, or any personal identification. You are completely anonymous when using Spendro.

No Analytics or Tracking

Spendro does not use any analytics services, crash reporting services, advertising networks, or tracking technologies. We have no visibility into how you use the app.

Advertising

The free version of Spendro may display ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads in accordance with Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy. You can permanently remove all ads by upgrading to Spendro Premium.

In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. All billing is handled entirely by Google or Apple under their respective terms of service.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your Spendro data at any time by uninstalling the app. All data is permanently and immediately removed when the app is uninstalled. There is no account to delete and no server-side data to request removal of.

Children's Privacy

Spendro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Spendro after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
